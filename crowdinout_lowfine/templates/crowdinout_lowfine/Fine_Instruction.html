As of now, you might realize that if everybody catches 20 fish, your group aggregate earnings would be maximized, so is the average group earnings. Therefore, at the beginning of this round {{round_nm}}, a regulation is introduced where the maximum allowable catch is 20 fish. By catching over 20 fish, the fish will be caught too fast. To enforce this regulation, there is a 5% chance that a player will be randomly audited and pay a fine of 2 points for each unit of extraction over the 20 fish.
