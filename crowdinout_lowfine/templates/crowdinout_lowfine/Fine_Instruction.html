As of now, you might realize that if everybody catches 20 fish, your group's aggregate (and average) earnings would be maximized. Therefore, at the beginning of this round, a regulation is introduced where the maximum allowable catch is 20 fish. By catching over 20 fish, the fish will be caught too fast (the fish in the lake will not be sustainable). To enforce this regulation, there is a 5% chance that a player will be randomly audited and have to pay a fine of 2 points for each unit of extraction over the 20 fish.
